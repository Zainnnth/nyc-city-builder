# Aesthetic Bible: Late-90s / Early-2000s NYC

## Core Mood

Urban pressure, warm summer haze, sodium streetlights, CRT-era UI overlays, ad-heavy streets, and skyline silhouettes.

## Palette Direction

## Is It Vaporwave?

Best label: "vaporwave-adjacent urban retro."

- Vaporwave traits present: nostalgic era coding, synthetic contrast, neon accents.
- Not pure vaporwave: lower pastel use, higher grit, darker city massing, less surreal emptiness.

## Visual Rules

- Keep base environments desaturated and heavy.
- Use bright accents only for signage, transport, and nightlife districts.
- Avoid clean futuristic gloss; favor chipped paint, grime, and analog texture.
- Prioritize skyline readability over decorative clutter.
